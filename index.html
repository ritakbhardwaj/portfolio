My Vision

My journey as a developer started when my father gifted me a second hand computer in class 7th. I did a lot of experiments on it. Sometimes I would ruin it too, but I would fix it myself. I had learnt basic computer during that period.

I have done a certification course in computer Applications when I was in +1 class. That course gave me information about various computer applications. After my +2 class I have done another certification course in office automations. In that course I have learned all the basic applications that are used in offices.

My interest in computer and applications increased further while learning new things. Then I decided that I would do my graduation in Bachelor of Computer Applications. One year after graduation, I thought of understanding real world problems and started doing 3 months internship in a multinational company in Noida. There I took deep practical knowledge about website designing and development. I worked on many projects on local machine and learned many new things.

Along with graduation, I was also doing 2 year Diploma in Hardware and Maintenance. In which I learned about the working of computer and how to handle hardware issues. I had to submit the project for the final year graduation. I have decided that I will make a project in web development. So I made this fully responsive and dynamic web portfolio. Now my graduation is about to end. I didn't want to waste time, that’s why I started doing the ReactJS course.

I am constantly working on myself and improving my skills. One day I will show myself as the best developer in the web development industry.
